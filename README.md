# Hospital-Emergency-Room-Analysis

## Hospital Emergency Room Performance Dashboard
An interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze emergency room operations and patient flow in hospitals. The dashboard presents key performance indicators such as patient volume, waiting time, satisfaction levels, and department referrals, helping hospital administrators monitor service efficiency and identify operational trends.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 Microsoft Excel – Used for creating the interactive dashboard and visualizations
* 📈 Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts – Used to summarize and analyze patient records
* 🎛 Excel Slicers – Used for dynamic filtering by month and year
* 📐 Data Visualization Techniques – Used to present healthcare data in an intuitive and easy-to-understand format

## 📂 Data Source
* Source: Hospital Emergency Room dataset
* The dataset includes:
  * Patient visit records
  * Waiting time information
  * Patient satisfaction scores
  * Age-group distribution
  * Gender-wise patient data
  * Department referrals
  * Monthly and yearly ER visit trends

## 🚀 Features / Highlights

### 🔹 Business Problem
Hospital emergency rooms handle large volumes of patient data daily, making it difficult for administrators to quickly analyze operational performance and service efficiency. Key questions include:

* How many patients visit the ER in a given period?
* What is the average waiting time for patients?
* Which departments receive the most referrals?
* What demographic groups visit the ER most frequently?

Without proper visualization, extracting these insights from spreadsheets becomes time-consuming.

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive dashboard that:

* Tracks patient volume and emergency room activity
* Monitors average waiting time and service efficiency
* Evaluates patient satisfaction levels
* Analyzes patient demographics such as age and gender
* Identifies departments with the highest referral rates

### 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 🔢 KPI Cards (Top Section)

* Total Patients: 493
* Average Wait Time: 34.93 minutes
* Patient Satisfaction Score: 4.74

#### 📊 Patient Attended Status (Pie Chart)

Displays the percentage of patients attended on time versus delayed, helping evaluate emergency room efficiency.

#### 🚻 Gender-wise Analysis (Donut Chart)

Shows the distribution of male and female patients visiting the emergency room.

#### 👶 Age-wise Group Analysis (Bar Chart)

Analyzes patient visits across different age groups to understand which demographics rely most on emergency services.

#### 🏥 Department Referral Analysis (Bar Chart)
Shows the number of patients referred to departments such as:
* General Practice
* Orthopedics
* Physiotherapy
* Neurology
* Cardiology
* Renal
* Gastroenterology

#### 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)
* Month filter
* Year filter

Allows users to dynamically explore emergency room data across different time periods.

## 📈 Business Impact & Insights
* Operational Monitoring :
Hospital administrators can quickly track patient inflow and service efficiency.

* 📊 Resource Planning :
Department referral data helps identify departments requiring more staff or resources.

* 👥 Demographic Understanding :
Age and gender analysis provide insights into patient population trends.

* ⭐ Service Quality Tracking :
Patient satisfaction scores help evaluate the quality of care provided in the emergency room.
